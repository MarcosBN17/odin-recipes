Milk-Shake Recipe



Description

Milkshake is a sweet beverage made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, or fruit syrup into a thick, sweet, cold mixture.

Ingredients

  • 4 large scoops of vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 c. milk
  • Whipped topping, for garnish Sprinkles, for garnish Maraschino cherry

Method

Step 1

In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk

Step 2

Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
