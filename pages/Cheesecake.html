Cheesecake Recipe



Description

Cheesecake is a dessert that consists of a thick, creamy filling of cheese, eggs, and sugar over a thinner crust and topped with sweet or sometimes salty items.

Ingredients

  • 250g digestive biscuits
  • 125g butter in room temperature
  • 600g soft cheese
  • 200ml double cream
  • handful strawberries or rasberries or any marmelade of your choice

Method

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 180C.

Step 2

Blend the digestive biscuits and the butter in a food processor until the misture looks like wet and sand. Then, press the mixture into the bottom of the tin and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Step 3

Cool the base of the cheesecake while preparing the filling

Step 4

Stir in a bowl the soft cheese and the caster sugar for a little. Continue by adding the double cream. Pour the mixture in the tin and smooth with a spoon or a spatula.

Step 5

Leave to set in the fridge for 1 hour or better overnight and serve with strawberries or raspberries or any marmelade you like.
